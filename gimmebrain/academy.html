The Notes Box

Every Module Has a Notes Field at the Bottom

It's a text box where you can write anything — questions, corrections, or new info you think should be in the training. Your notes get logged with your name and module number, and Gayden reviews them every morning at 9:30 AM.

❓
Ask a Question
Confused about something in the module? Just ask. Gayden will write an answer and update the training if it's unclear for everyone.
"What do we do if the realtor says the seller already has a cash offer?"
🔧
Flag a Correction
If something is wrong or outdated — a script, a number, a quiz answer — call it out. Reference the pool index number for quiz questions.
"Pool Q14 says the DD period in Texas is 10 days but it should be based on the option period."
💡
Suggest New Info
Picked up something on a call that should be in the training? Share it. If it checks out, it gets added to the KB and the module page.
"Realtors in Henderson keep asking about our proof of funds — we should add a section on how to handle that."
💬
Anything Else
Feedback on the quiz difficulty, requests for more examples, thoughts on the module structure — all fair game. There are no wrong notes.
"Module 3 could use a real example of a counter-offer email thread."
● Notes are reviewed daily. When Gayden acts on one, the KB, quiz pool, and module page all update together automatically.
