How the Notes Box Works

Your Feedback Powers Updates

At the bottom of every module, there's a text box where you can submit questions, corrections, or new information. When referencing a specific quiz question, use the pool index number—for example, Pool Q14—rather than the question's position in your current attempt.

What happens next: Your notes are logged with your name and the module. Every morning at 9:30 AM, Gayden reviews them in a queue. If it's a question, he'll write an answer. If it's a correction or new info, he'll update the training material — the KB, the quiz questions, and the live module page all get updated together automatically.

Pro tip: Be specific. "Q14 is wrong" is helpful. "The answer to Pool Q14 should be X because Y" is even better—it helps Gayden fix it faster and makes sure the update is exactly right.

All notes are reviewed and processed daily. Check the module page to see when updates have been made based on feedback from you and your teammates.
