Good Results Academy
● 12 MODULES
Start here. 12 modules covering the entire Good Results operation — pipeline management, cold calling scripts, offer process, post-contract SOPs, SMS playbook, comps, closing, contracts, financing, due diligence, and foreclosures. Each module has a reading section, a quiz pool that tracks which questions you have mastered (so you stop seeing ones you already know), and a notes box where you can flag mistakes, ask questions, or suggest new content. Gayden reviews notes daily and updates the training. Your scores and progress show on the team leaderboard. Work through them in order — each one builds on the last.
🔍
KB Search
● LIVE
Search across every Good Results knowledge base at once — Academy training modules, call analyzer playbook, Chloe voice agent scripts, and SMS cadences. Type any keyword or phrase and results come back color-coded by source so you know where the info lives. If you search for something and the answer is not detailed enough, use the question box at the bottom to send it to the questions-and-answers Slack channel. Joe and Gayden get tagged automatically and will respond.
🎙
Call Analyzer
● LIVE
After every call, paste the transcript here. AI scores it across 7 criteria using the Good Results playbook — buy box delivery, objection handling, tone and professionalism, forbidden language, closing attempt, and follow-up setup. You get a detailed breakdown of what you did right, what you missed, and the exact script you should have used at each missed moment. Every call analyzed gets added to the knowledge base, so the scoring gets smarter over time. Use this on every single call — not just the ones that went badly.
🚀
Deal Launcher
● LIVE
When you go under contract, this is your first stop. Upload the signed contract and property photos, fill in the deal details, and hit Launch. It creates the deal page on the website, updates the opportunity in Close CRM, and generates a full disposition workflow with AI-written texts and emails pulled from the SMS playbook. One button replaces what used to be 45 minutes of manual setup across three different systems.

Deal Dashboard
● LIVE
Live view of every active deal across Las Vegas, Reno, and Houston. See pipeline status, contract dates, EMD deadlines, and disposition progress at a glance. Use this every morning to know what needs attention today — which deals are approaching deadlines, which dispositions need follow-up, and where the pipeline stands across all three markets.
