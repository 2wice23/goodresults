Drop The Deal. Launch The Page.

Upload a contract, enter your numbers, add photos, and hit one button. It builds the deal page on the website, creates a dispo workflow in Close, and gives you a live URL — all in one shot.

What It Does
One Button, Three Things
Extracts property data from your contract PDF using AI. Pushes a deal page (template + data + photos) straight to GitHub which auto-deploys to the live site. Then creates a dispo text/email workflow in Close CRM — named after the address, ready to activate.
How To Use It
Fill the Left, Watch the Right
Upload the contract PDF and hit Extract. Fill in wholesale price, ARV, and repairs per sqft. Upload your deal photos and drag them into the order you want. Confirm the deal page ID. Hit "Launch Deal" — done. The right panel shows progress and your live link when it's ready.
What Gets Created
Live Page + Close Workflow
Your deal page goes live at goodresults.org/deals/[deal-id]. In Close, you get a draft workflow with: teaser SMS, deal-link email (both instant), deal-link SMS (2hrs later), and a follow-up SMS (next day). Just activate it and enroll contacts.
