Drop The Deal. Launch The Page.

Upload a contract, review and edit all extracted fields, enter your numbers, add photos, then use the action buttons to push each piece — deal folder, photos, Close CRM, and dispo workflow — one step at a time.

What It Does
Four Buttons, Full Control
Extracts property data from your contract PDF using AI. Every field is editable — review and tweak before pushing. Create the deal folder on GitHub, push photos, update Close CRM, and create the dispo workflow — each with its own button.
How To Use It
Extract → Edit → Push
Upload the contract PDF and hit Extract. All fields appear as editable inputs on the right — fix anything Claude missed. Fill in your numbers (price, ARV, repairs). Upload photos and drag to reorder. Then use the action buttons in order: Create Folder → Push Photos → Push to Close → Create Workflow.
What Gets Created
Live Page + Close Workflow
Your deal page goes live at goodresults.org/deals/[deal-id]. In Close, you get a 6-step dispo workflow: Day 1 — teaser SMS + email (instant), then deal-link SMS (46 min). Day 2 — follow-up SMS + email, then final nudge SMS (2 hrs). Just activate it and enroll contacts.
