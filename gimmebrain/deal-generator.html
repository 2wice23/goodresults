✓ deal-doc.txt Downloaded — Here's What To Do

  1. Open deal-doc.txt in Notepad — fill in dealId, askingPrice, arv, estRepairs and save
  2. Create a new folder anywhere and name it whatever you want (e.g. 123mainst)
  3. Copy _template.html into the folder, rename it index.html
  4. Drop deal-doc.txt + your photos (01.jpg, 02.jpg...) into the same folder
  5. Upload the folder to Netlify → live at goodresults.org/your-folder-name
